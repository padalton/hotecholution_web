5. General Information on Processing Your Personal Data

When you visit this website, your IP address, session data, and technical information are automatically captured as part of the normal operation of the server. This constitutes a legitimate organisational interest. Data entry on any forms on this website remains entirely voluntary; any consent you provide can be revoked at any time.

The legal bases for processing your personal data include: your consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR), the performance of a contract (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR), compliance with a legal obligation (Art. 6(1)(c) GDPR), legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR), and, where applicable, § 96 TKG.

6. No Tracking, No Analytics

This website does not use Google Analytics, Google Tag Manager, or any third-party analytics or tracking service. The site loads no resources from third-party domains at runtime: fonts are self-hosted, there are no embedded social widgets, and no marketing pixels are present.

We may use Cloudflare Web Analytics, which is cookieless and stores no personal data (aggregate request statistics only). This does not require consent under the ePrivacy Directive or the Austrian Telekommunikationsgesetz.

Cloudflare acts as our edge/CDN provider and may set the strictly-necessary cookie __cf_bm for bot management (see Cloudflare's privacy page). Hetzner Online GmbH (DE) hosts our mail server under a separate data-processing agreement.

No personal data is stored in browser cookies by this site. The site may store the user-selected language preference in localStorage (key hto-lang) as a strictly-necessary preference; this is removed entirely once the language is determined by URL.

7. Your Rights Regarding Personal Data

Under the GDPR you have the following rights:

  • The right to access information about your stored personal data
  • The right to have inaccurate data corrected
  • The right to request erasure of your data
  • The right to restrict processing of your data
  • The right to data portability
  • The right to object to processing

Any consent you have previously granted can be revoked at any time without affecting the lawfulness of processing carried out prior to revocation. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the details provided in section 3 above.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Austrian Data Protection Authority (Datenschutzbehörde — DSB), Wickenburggasse 8–10, 1080 Vienna, Austria.

8. Server Log Files

Our web server automatically collects and stores information in server log files that your browser transmits. This includes: browser type and version, operating system, referrer URL, IP address, and the date and time of the server request.

This data cannot be attributed to specific individuals and is not combined with other data sources. The legal basis for processing is our legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR) in maintaining a technically secure and operational website, and evidence preservation pursuant to Art. 17(3)(e) GDPR. Log data is reviewed retrospectively only if there is a concrete indication of unlawful activity.

9. Storage Location and Duration

Data submitted via contact forms is retained for six months for the purpose of handling your request and necessary follow-up. Our hosting provider, Hetzner Online GmbH, retains server log data for 14 days; backup copies may be retained by backup providers for up to five years. No personal data is shared with third parties without your consent.
