Why apaleo, and only apaleo

An API-first PMS deserves API-first apps.

apaleo is the cloud-native, open PMS that lets a small studio build deeply integrated apps — not CSV add-ons. Four reasons every HoTechOlution product is apaleo-only:

API-first design

apaleo's OpenAPI exposes folio state, reservations, payment events and multi-property isolation on a single surface. That's what lets our apps embed into apaleo One in real time — not as nightly batch syncs.

Real-time webhooks

apaleo Pay and reservation webhooks fire the moment something happens. Cases close in seconds; data stays in sync — not in tomorrow morning's reconciliation.

Multi-property by design

Every product respects per-property configuration: rules, exclusions, mappings and grace periods are independent. A 3-hotel group and a 30-hotel chain configure the same way.

EU-hosted, GDPR by design

Vienna-based, EU-hosted, built by a Geprüfter Datenschutzexperte. Same legal jurisdiction as our DACH hotel customers. No US sub-processors in the request path.

Frequently asked

Straight answers.

The six questions we hear most often. If yours isn't here, write Stefan directly.

What does "apaleo-native" actually mean?

Every HoTechOlution product is built directly on apaleo's OpenAPI and live webhooks. Cases close in seconds, not on tomorrow's batch run, and the UI embeds inside apaleo One. No CSV exports, no nightly syncs, no "integration partners" in the middle.

Who builds and supports the software?

Stefan Wölflinger — founder, sole developer, and the person who answers your email. 4+ years on the apaleo OpenAPI, 15+ years in software development, certified Datenschutzexperte in Vienna.

Are your apps GDPR-compliant?

Yes. Built in Europe, hosted in the EU, designed by a Geprüfter Datenschutzexperte. Each customer's data is isolated; payment links use Apaleo Pay; no US sub-processors in the request path. We can also run a full GDPR audit on your apaleo stack — see Consulting.

What does an apaleo BMD accounting export cost?

€20 per hotel per month, single tier, all features included. Existing BMD Connector customers keep their original price for life.

How is GIMSI different from a generic CRM or group tool?

GIMSI lives inside apaleo One in 11 places. Group profiles, rate selection, offer PDFs and reservation pushback all happen against your real apaleo data in real time — not in a separate system that forgets to sync.

What's the difference between Light and Enterprise pricing?

Tiers differ by property size only — never by features. Smaller hotels pay less because they're smaller, not because the product is crippled. Above the Light caps you switch to per-room Enterprise pricing automatically.
